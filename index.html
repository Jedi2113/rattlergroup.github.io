Introducing Rattler

Rattler is a team of individuals committed to growth—in skill, in discipline, and in leadership. We challenge ourselves and each other to be better: sharper thinkers, stronger teammates, and more capable in the face of real-world adversity. Our mission is simple: To train and prepare ourselves to protect our families, friends, and communities in times of crisis. Our vision is clear: To create a network of trained, self-sufficient individuals ready for any challenge. Rattler isn’t a game, a gimmick, or a reenactment group. We don’t posture, and we’re not looking for a fight. We train hard, push each other, and operate with purpose. We believe in service over status—and when possible, we turn our skills outward. Whether it’s volunteering in the aftermath of a disaster, running supplies, cleaning up neighborhoods, or simply showing up for those who need help, we are here to support our communities, not just ourselves. If you’re ready to grow, learn, and lead—Rattler is here. Welcome to the challenge.

Semper Promptus

Our slogan, Semper Promptus, Latin for "Always Ready", captures the core of what Rattler stands for. It’s not just a phrase, but a commitment we live by. Through rigorous training, discipline, and teamwork, we prepare ourselves to respond swiftly and effectively in times of crisis, whether it’s a natural disaster, a community emergency, or a moment when someone simply needs help. Being "Always Ready" means we train with purpose, stay equipped, and build the mindset to step up when others can’t. It's a reminder that readiness isn't just about gear, it's about heart, reliability, and showing up for the people who depend on us.



Step into Something Real

Rattler is more than a group, it’s a commitment to growth, service, and preparedness. We challenge one another to become more capable, more confident, and more ready to help when it matters most. Whether it’s through skill-building, team development, or stepping up in times of need, we’re creating something that lasts. This isn’t about conflict, it’s about community. If you’re ready to grow, lead, and serve with purpose, we’re ready for you.

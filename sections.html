The 188th Division "Gaslighters"

Infantry

The infantry is the backbone of any military unit, literally holding the line and standing firm in the face of any challenge. As an infantryman, you’ll be at the forefront of operations, mastering advanced weapons systems, navigation, and tactical maneuvers. This role demands sharp decision-making, and teamwork, offering unmatched opportunities for personal growth and leadership development. If you’re ready to stand strong, make a tangible impact, and forge unbreakable bonds with your team, the infantry is where you belong.



Recon/h1>

The recon team is an extension of the infantry, specializing in gathering critical intelligence and operating deep behind enemy lines. With limited and competitive slots, joining the recon team requires exceptional skill, a sharp memory, and expertise in land navigation. As a recon team member, you’ll master stealth, precision, and adaptability, becoming the eyes and ears of the unit. If you’re ready to push your limits, hold the line with unparalleled focus, and excel in one of the most challenging and rewarding roles, the recon team is where you belong.



Armor Section

Armor is the spearhead of modern warfare, combining heavy firepower, cutting-edge technology, and unmatched mobility to dominate the battlefield. As part of the armor team, you’ll operate and maintain armored vehicles, taking on roles that range from crewman to strategist. Whether leading the charge or providing crucial support, you’ll play a key role in shaping mission outcomes and protecting your fellow soldiers. If you’re ready to bring the fight to the enemy, hold the line with unyielding strength, and be a driving force in any operation, armor is the place for you.



Air Wing

The air wing delivers unmatched agility, precision, and support to every mission, providing critical capabilities from the skies. Openings in the air wing are rare and highly competitive, offering a chance to be part of a team operating and maintaining advanced aircraft. Whether piloting, navigating, or ensuring these cutting-edge machines stay mission-ready, you’ll play an essential role in missions ranging from air superiority to close air support and logistics. If you’re ready to rise above the rest, hold the line from above, and embrace a truly exceptional challenge, the air wing is where you belong.
